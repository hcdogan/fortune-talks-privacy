Information Collection

Fortune Talks does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We literally collect NOTHING. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Third-Party Services

Fortune Talks uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases. When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is processed entirely by Apple
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • We do not have access to your credit card details
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions
  • View Apple's privacy policy at: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

No other third-party services are used. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising networks.

In-App Purchases

Fortune Talks offers optional premium coin themes:

  • Silver Coin Theme - $0.99 USD
  • Gold Coin Theme - $1.99 USD

When you purchase:

  • The transaction is handled by Apple's App Store
  • Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID (managed by Apple)
  • We only receive confirmation that a purchase was made
  • We cannot see your payment details or personal information
  • You can restore purchases on other devices using the same Apple ID

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using:

  • UserDefaults - A standard iOS/watchOS data storage system
  • Stored on your device only
  • Not transmitted anywhere
  • Not backed up to our servers (we don't have servers!)
  • May be backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud backup enabled (controlled by Apple)

Data stored locally:

  • Number of coin flips (heads vs tails count)
  • Selected coin theme (Bronze, Silver, or Gold)
  • Purchase status (which themes you've unlocked)

This data:

  • Never leaves your watch
  • Is not sent to us or anyone else
  • Can be deleted by removing the app
  • Can be reset using the in-app reset button

Data Security

Since we don't collect, transmit, or store any user data on our servers (we don't even have servers), there is no risk of your personal information being:

  • Accessed by us
  • Sold to third parties
  • Breached in a data leak
  • Shared with advertisers
  • Used for profiling

Your data stays on your device, under your control.
