How to Use

  1. Tap the coin to flip it and watch the smooth 3D animation
  2. View Statistics by tapping the chart icon to see your flip history
  3. Choose Themes by tapping the palette icon to customize your coin
  4. Reset Statistics anytime from the Statistics screen

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new coin themes?
Tap the palette icon on the main screen to browse available themes. Premium themes can be unlocked through in-app purchase. The classic theme is always free!
Is the coin flip truly random?
Yes! Fortune Flips uses the device's secure random number generator to ensure completely fair and unpredictable results.
Can I use Fortune Flips on my Apple Watch?
Absolutely! Fortune Flips includes a full-featured Apple Watch app that you can use independently of your iPhone.
How do I restore my previous purchases?
If you've purchased themes on another device or need to restore them, tap the palette icon, scroll to the bottom, and select "Restore Purchases."
Will my statistics sync between devices?
Statistics are currently stored locally on each device. This ensures your flip history remains private and secure.
Can I turn off haptic feedback?
Haptic feedback is enabled by default to enhance the flipping experience. If you prefer to disable it, please reach out through our support contact below.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Fortune Flips respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All statistics and preferences are stored locally on your device.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase history is managed through your Apple ID.

Analytics

Fortune Flips does not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools. Your usage of the app remains completely private.

Children's Privacy

Fortune Flips is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
